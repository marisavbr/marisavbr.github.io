Curry Chicken : The Woks of Life + Dinner then Dessert

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

I referenced these two recipes to make the curry chicken. I was very happy with the outcome of combining the recipes. I would like to try it with coconut milk/cream as a way to make it a bit more rich. Overall, I was satisfied with the meal and will be making this again in the near future.

Notes :

1. Cut chicken into bite-sized bits (~1 lb) + toss in soy sauce and corn starch mixture.

2. Cook the chicken in wok. Once chicken is cooked, remove from wok.

3. Add more oil + cook veggies (onion, carrot, peppers).

4. Add ~1/4 cup chicken stock. Let it cook off (do this to keep the veggies from sticking to the pan).

5. Add the chicken stock from recipe (from thewoksoflife.com ).

6. Add spices + *brown sugar* (I basically ended up doubling the spices; I used curry powder and turmeric from thewoksoflife.com ).

7. Let the chicken stock cook down a bit, and then add corn starch slurry to thicken sauce. (I added two slurries to get desired thickness; 1 teaspoon corn starch : 2 teaspoons water and another 1:1).

8. Add the chicken back to the wok and let cook for a few minutes.

9. Serve with rice!

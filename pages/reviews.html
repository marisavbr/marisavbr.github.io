music

Hinge - E B U

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
my favorites : Falling, Light Show, Creatures, By & By, Sloth, Homeland

Hinge holds a consistent atmosphere through out the entirety of the album. With the help of this consistency,
E B U was able to create a cohesive sounding album while picking varying styles of sounds to experiement with.
This album ranges from having songs filled with doodles of sound to songs that are more oriented towards quirky
vocals/rhythms. Homeland shares similar characteristics to songs uses in the show Kaiba . This song could have
been a good last song as I feel it could have wrapped up the album nicely. Tagged as swamppop on bandcamp.

ideal listening environment : 7 feet underwater
realistic conditions : on a walk from twilight → dusk

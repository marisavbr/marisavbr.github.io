Can Opener - Great Grandpa

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
my favorites : Mostly Here, Ram, Cheeto Lust

I have listened to this EP so much since I found it (02-25-2021). I basically have it on repeat. More specifically, I ~religiously~ listen to Mostly Here. The sounds of the vocals stimulate such a strong physiological response in me that it makes me cry (this is especially heightened if I am attempting to sing it). There is an alternate version of this song on the album Four of Arrows . Can Opener is one of the few collections of songs that I can listen through without wanting to skip anything.

ideal listening environment : live at a show
realistic conditions : driving with the windows down

Hinge - E B U

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
my favorites : Homeland, Falling, By & By, Light Show, Creatures, Sloth

Hinge holds a consistent atmosphere through out the entirety of the album. With the help of this consistency, E B U was able to create a cohesive sounding album while picking varying styles of sounds to experiement with. This album ranges from having songs filled with doodles of sound to songs that are more oriented towards quirky vocals/rhythms. Homeland shares similar characteristics to songs used through out the show Kaiba . Additionally, this song could have been a good last song as I felt it would have wrapped up the album nicely. Tagged as swamppop on bandcamp.

ideal listening environment : 7 feet underwater
realistic conditions : on a walk from twilight → dusk
